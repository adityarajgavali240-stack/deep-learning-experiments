# Case Study — Transformer Architecture\n\n## Aim\nUnderstanding the Transformer Architecture: Revolutionizing Sequence Modeling.\n\n## Theory\nThe Transformer architecture, introduced by Vaswani et al. in 2017 in 'Attention Is All You Need', marked a paradigm shift in deep learning. Unlike traditional RNNs, Transformers rely entirely on the self-attention mechanism, allowing for parallel computation and state-of-the-art performance across NLP and vision tasks.
