# Case Study — Attention Mechanism\n\n## Aim\nAn interactive study of the Attention mechanism in Deep Learning.\n\n## Theory\nThe attention mechanism is one of the most transformative developments in deep learning, especially for sequential data such as text, speech, and images. Initially introduced to enhance sequence-to-sequence (Seq2Seq) models in natural language processing (NLP), attention enables models to focus selectively on relevant parts of the input when generating each output element.
